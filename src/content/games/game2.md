Sonic the Hedgehog is a renowned platform game series developed and published by Sega, first released in 1991. The franchise stars Sonic, a blue anthropomorphic hedgehog known for his incredible speed, as he battles the evil Dr. Robotnik (also known as Dr. Eggman) to save his animal friends and collect Chaos Emeralds.

The original game introduced several defining features:

- Fast-paced side-scrolling gameplay
- Loop-de-loops and dynamic level design
- Collectible golden rings for protection and points
- Unique power-ups and shield abilities

Sonic's debut set a new standard for platformers, emphasizing speed and fluid movement, which distinguished it from other games of its era.

## Key Features

- High-speed platforming action
- Vibrant, colorful environments
- Memorable soundtrack and sound effects
- Iconic characters like Tails and Knuckles

The Sonic series has left a lasting legacy, inspiring numerous sequels, spin-offs, and adaptations across various media, and remains a beloved part of gaming history.
